About Service Record

One of the important parts of school learning is to nurture and skills and proper attitudes about serving others in the community.

Every year, each student needs to enter the records of her/his services in the school year for teachers' review and endorsement.

At the end of each school year, each student is awarded accordingly based on her/his service hours.

Students who have served for at least 100 hours would obtain Gold award.

Students who have served for at least 50 hours but less than 100 hours would obtain Silver award.

Students who have served for at least 25 hours but less than 50 hours would obtain Bronze award.

Students who have served for at least 10 hours but less than 25 hours would obtain Merit award.
